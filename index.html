Safety Training Services

Future Environment Designs offers safety training and respirator fittings at our offices, at your site and online, in the following areas:



Asbestos Training

Our New York State certified safety training covers all aspects of asbestos removal, including Project Design, Supervisor and Operations & Management, as well as Air Sampling and Allied Trades.

OSHA Training

We offer OSHA safety training in Lead Safety, 10/30 hour Construction Safety, Hazardous Waste Removal, Respirable Silica, Bloodborne Pathogens, Emergency Response, and more!

Mold Training

We offer courses in Mold Abatement, Remediation, and Supervisor, applying our 25+ years of experience to provide you with the most up-to-date safety knowledge for use on the job.

Respirator Services

We can help you write an OSHA-compliant respiratory protection program, help you meet fit testing and medical evaluation requirements, and instruct your employees on proper use and care of their Half Face, Full Face, PAPRs, Dust Masks, or Filtering Facepiece respirators.

Online Training

Future Environment Designs, Inc. now brings the safety training right to your desktop via our e-Learning courses. Just look for the classes designated by the eLearn- prefix.
